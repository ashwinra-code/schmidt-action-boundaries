# Limitations And Claim Boundary

## Supported

The retrospective proof-of-concept supports a bounded dense-posture target-surface recovery claim in k=5 triage response surfaces.

## Not Supported

- sparse high-harm recovery
- prospective urology validity
- active-search success
- repair success
- k=10 scaling
- universal low-degree structure
- population-level inference over all frontier models

## Wording Constraint

Allowed wording should stay close to:

> In retrospective k=5 triage response surfaces, a dense posture endpoint (`acceptable_floor_choice`) showed above-baseline hidden-cell recovery from q=12 observed cells.

Avoid wording that implies:

- harmful failures were recovered
- the result transfers to urology
- degree-1 is universally best
- the model's full policy was recovered
- the proof-of-concept establishes prospective evaluation validity
